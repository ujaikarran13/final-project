Application Features

  • Create and manage personal, private bookmarks for your favorite websites.
  • Share bookmarks with other users by marking them as "public".
  • Report any public bookmarks you find inappropriate.
  • Explore public bookmarks shared by other users.

Community Guidelines

The following set of Community Guidelines outline the type of content and behavior we expect from our user community. These policies apply to all types of content, including usernames, links, images, and comments. The Community Guidelines are regularly updated based on customer feedback and the input of outside experts to stay current with the most recent challenges.

Our aim is to ensure this application is inclusive and safe, and a valuable resource for our user community. To support this, these Community Guidelines are enforced by our administrative staff, with the assistance of the user community. These policies are applied equally to everyone, regardless of subject or users background, political views, or affiliations.

We expect all members of our community to:

  • Share fun and useful content.
  • Treat others with respect, acting with maturity and consideration for others.
  • Respect the privacy and personal information of others.
  • Keep content relevant and in good taste.
  • Report any content which violates these guidelines.

We will take action against any content that is:

  • illegal
  • offensive (racist, sexist, or otherwise)
  • explicit or pornographic
  • hateful or inflammatory
  • spam, inauthentic or misleading

Bookmark Visibility

Bookmarks may be either private or public.

  • Private bookmarks are only visible to you. The application does not permit anyone else to view or edit your Private bookmarks.
  • Public bookmarks are visible to all users of the application. Bookmark Manager administrative staff moderate Public bookmarks, and may edit or delete them as appropriate for the overall experience of the community.

When creating a new bookmark, you can control access to your bookmark by setting the visibility.

If you decide later that you want to change the visibility of one of your own bookmarks, you may do this from the My Bookmarks page. There are two ways to do this:

  1. Click the Visibility icon for the bookmark.
  2. Click the Edit icon for the bookmark, then select the desired visibility using the select control.

User Profile

All users have a public profile which is referenced from any Public Bookmarks shared. Profile information is optional, but gives other members of the community an opportunity to learn a little about you. This profile includes:

  • your display name if provided, otherwise your username is used
  • your profile image
  • an optional, short paragraph about you, labeled "Short bio"
  • a list of all Public Bookmarks you have shared

This information is editable from "My Profile".
